Sun Yifan

A filmmaker based in Warsaw, Poland.

I am currently a senior student major in Film Directing at Lodz Film School. Over the years, I have gained extensive practical and interpersonal skills about film-making, and in combination with the theoretical knowledge I had developed during my previous studies at BFA, I successfully completed some short film projects.

My fascination with reading and traveling has broadened my horizon and enabled me to view things from different perspectives, to embrace diversities and to respect and communicate with people that have opposite opinions. Coming from a mixed culture myself, I have great empathy towards minority groups and my investigative nature drives me to explore the unknown and discover the underneath.
