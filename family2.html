STORY

Lola was adopted into a Belgian family eleven years ago when she was an infant. Over the years, she has been imagining the reunion with her birth family in China. This summer, her dream finally comes true. When Lola's two families finally meet for the first time, how will they spend the time together? What will Lola's birth parents tell her?

CREW

DIRECTOR

YIFAN SUN

A director and writer based in Warsaw and Beijing.

Yifan was born and raised in Inner Mongolia, having graduated from Beijing Film Academy (BFA), she continued her adventure with films. She is currently pursuing her M.A. in Film Directing at Lodz Film School in Poland. Her films have been selected at worldwide film festivals/awards, e.g. InsideOut, student BAFTA, and the Student Academy Awards. Now, she is working on her feature debut A Journey Back Home.

CINEMATOGRAPHER

ALICIA DEVAUX

An aspiring French cinematographer who is specialised in documentary films.

Alicia holds a Master of Arts and is currently a Phd student at Lodz Film School. She has various experiences in working with international directors and has worked in countries across Europe, Asia and Middle-East. The short films she worked on were selected at major film festivals and recieved prestigious awards.
