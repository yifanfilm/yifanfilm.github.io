STORY

Lola was adopted into a Belgian family eleven years ago when she was an infant. Over the years, she has been imagining the reunion with her birth family in China. This summer, her dream finally comes true. When Lola's two families finally meet for the first time, how will they spend the time together? What will Lola's birth parents tell her?

CREW

DIRECTOR

YIFAN SUN

Director and writer based in Warsaw and Beijing.

Yifan was born and raised in Inner Mongolia, having graduated from Beijing Film Academy (BFA), she continued her adventure with films. She is currently pursuing her M.A. in Film Directing at Lodz Film School in Poland. Her films have been selected to worldwide film festivals/awards, including InsideOut, student BAFTA.

CINEMATOGRAPHER

ALICIA DEVAUX

EDITOR

DOROTA ROŚ

Dorota Roś was born in Warsaw, Poland. Since 2014 she is enrolled in Lodz Film School at the editing faculty.

As an editor, she has already worked on several short films, both fiction and documentary. She has also experience as an editor’s assistant and editor for television production. Films edited by Dorota were presented at dozen of international film festivals worldwide and awarded with important awards.
